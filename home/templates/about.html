Rev up your possibilities! A one-stop destination for car owners and seekers alike. The SMACarRental System is a user-friendly platform that allows individuals to rent out their cars and also rent cars from others. The system is designed to provide a seamless experience for both car owners and renters. It offers features such as generating invoices, adding/editing cars, managing user profiles.

Key Features:

  • Car Rental and Listing:
    • Car owners can list their vehicles on the platform with detailed descriptions, images, and rental rates.
    • Renters can search for available cars based on their preferred location, date, and vehicle type.
  • Booking and Reservation:
    • Renters can easily book a car of their choice by selecting the desired dates and making a payment through a secure payment gateway.
    • Car owners receive booking requests and can accept or reject them based on availability.
  • Invoice Generation:
    • The system automatically generates detailed invoices for each rental transaction, including rental charges, duration, taxes, and any additional fees.
    • Renters can view and download invoices from their profiles for their records.
  • Car Management:
    • Car owners can add new vehicles to the platform, providing essential details such as make, model, year, fuel type, and insurance information.
    • They can also edit existing car listings to update availability, rental rates, or other vehicle specifications.
  • User Profiles:
    • Both car owners and renters have individual user profiles where they can manage their personal information, contact details, and preferences.
    • Renters can view their rental history and saved cars, while car owners can track their bookings and earnings.
